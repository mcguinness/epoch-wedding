When & Where

Wedding Ceremony & Party

Saturday June 22, 2019
The Julia Morgan Ballroom 465 California St San Francisco, CA

The Julia Morgan Ballroom was San Francisco's first skyscraper and one of the only buildings to survive the 1906 earthquake. It is named after California's first female architect; a woman who graduated from Oakland High School, earned a civil engineering degree from U.C. Berkeley in 1894 (go Bears!) and designed over 700 buildings throughout California, including Hearst Castle. The Julia Morgan Ballroom symbolizes the longevity and eternality that Karl and Kori-Renée wish for their own marriage.
